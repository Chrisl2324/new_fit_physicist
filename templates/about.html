About Me

Fitness and Physics...well mostly Fitness ...but a little bit of physics....


How it Began

My fitness journey began at a young age. Growing up, I was very active and had a very athletic group of friends who had an enormous influence in how I chose to spend my time. This usually including outdoor sports and then gradually grew into weightlifting when I entered high school. Athough it was more of a hobby back in those days, it has since become an intense passion of mine. It has continued to grow since then, and I have devoted a great deal of my time to perfecting my workout routines, while also learning as much as I could from online resources, and in-person with other fitness professionals. Weightlifting, however, has kind of taken a back seat to my new growing passion: calisthenics. Because of this, my exercise style includes a combination of styles, focusing on body-weight training, but also incorporating heavy lifting to maintain muscle mass and build the best body possible.

Life Happens

After high school, I received an academic scholarship to attend the University of Miami. I thought I had everything figured out and that I would have a smooth road to success and happiness. Life had other plans for me. In my twenties, I started seeking help for my growing depression, which seemed to come about with no apparent cause. Although I noticed marginal improvements, I eventually turned to drugs to self-medicate which turned out to be the lynchpin in the next 10 or so tumoltuous years of my life. I dropped out of college, moved back in with my parents, and spent many years with no direction at all, ultimately ending up in prison for a crime related to my drug use. I have been very open with anyone I meet about my current situation (at the time of writing this I am in a half-way house looking foward to my release in the next 6 weeks), because it says a lot about my character and my ability to continue on despite life's challenges. I also mention this because, although the main goal of creating this website is to help anyone interested in fitness, I also want to inspire others to pursue self-improvement even if they are facing adversity. Change can and does happen when hard work is put in!
